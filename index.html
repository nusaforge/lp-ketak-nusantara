02 — Our Story

Born from a
family
legacy.

It started in 1999, when Dian's parents — Suhartono & Mawarianti — opened Mawar Art Shop in Lingsar, West Lombok.

In 2021, their daughter Dian Eka Purnama Sari — scholarship recipient from Indonesia's Ministry of Industry — founded Ketak Nusantara.

"If not me as the first daughter, who will carry this legacy forward?"

04 — The Founder

Dian Eka
Purnama Sari

The youngest female entrepreneur in West Nusa Tenggara. Textile scholar funded by Indonesia's Ministry of Industry scholarship.

She leads a community of 500-600 women artisans in Lingsar, bringing Lombok heritage to global markets.

"Keep trying — either you win or you learn. We'll never know if we succeed before we start."
